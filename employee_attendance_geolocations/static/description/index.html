Track Employees Location with Ease!!

Managing employee attendances manually becomes prone to errors. Attendance geolocation helps the admin to track the employee at real-time.

Employee attendance geolocation reduce the chances of inaccuracy and helps you to save the time of tracking employee location manually one-by-one.

This type of modules are mostly preferred by the organization where the employees have some field work but in this era of WFH, admin can also manage their employees location.

When the employees are working remotely, there are a lot of chances that the employees are working reliantly. With Employees Attendance Geolocation, Hr can monitor the employees remotely.

In the employee field job, attendance geolocation is necessary for the employers to calculate the expenses of fuels, tickets, etc.

Employee attendance Geolocation also helps the employers to calculate the timesheets and working hours of their employees accurately and allows the employers to pay their employees for what they are working.

Detailed Features List

Below is the list of Feature for Odoo Employee Attendance Geolocation

Track Employees Attendance

  • Managers can check the employees check-in/check-out location.
  • Manager can check the location of the employees individually using filters.

Employee Attendance Geolocation Configuration

  • Enable/Disable Required Employee Location.
  • Set allowed the User group to access geolocation features.
  • Allow address on the map marker.
  • Used to create a route from the check-in to check-out point on the google map.

View Attendance Location

  • Allowed User groups can check the employee location.
  • Employees can also check their check-in and check-out location.
  • Tree view button to check the employee attendance location.

Mark Attendance

  • Employees can mark check-in and check-out for the day with their real-time location.
  • Admin can also check the difference between the distances in check-in and check-out location.
